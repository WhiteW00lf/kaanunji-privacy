Privacy Policy – Kaanunji

Last updated: 06 February 2026

Kaanunji respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the Kaanunji mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Questions entered into the app
  • A randomly generated device identifier (device_id)
  • Usage count for enforcing free usage limits

We do not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to:

  • Provide general legal information responses
  • Enforce free and paid usage limits
  • Improve performance through caching
  • Prevent misuse and ensure platform security

Legal Disclaimer

Kaanunji provides general legal information only and does not offer legal advice. For advice specific to your situation, consult a qualified advocate.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. User queries may be processed by third-party AI services solely for the purpose of generating responses.

Data Security & Retention

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored data. Usage logs and cached responses may be retained as required for service operation and abuse prevention.

Children’s Privacy

Kaanunji is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age.
